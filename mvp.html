Be First to Experience the CastReady Tween Pre Order Boxes!

Early access for families and programs in New England and the Tri State area. Limited quantities—reserve your box and help shape the future of acting education.

Course Description

We’re launching our very first CastReady boxes for tweens (ages 9–12) and inviting families and programs in New England and the Tri State area (NY, NJ, CT) to join us as early adopters. This Pre Order launch is your chance to get hands on, screen light acting resources, provide feedback, and become part of the CastReady founding community.

How It Works
  • Choose your box and complete your pre order online (shipping only to New England and Tri State area).
  • Receive your box at home—shipping begins
  • Try it out: Use the materials with your tween, then share your feedback with CastReady.
  • Stay connected: Get early updates on future boxes, digital features, and national expansion.
Your Pre Order Tween Box
  • All materials are screen light and hands on — no extra tech required.
  • Every box includes clear instructions for tweens and parents.
  • All boxes come with foundational flashcards and worksheets.
  • Co branded boxes include limited digital access to select Callback Zone resources via QR code (full digital subscription not included in Pre Order).
