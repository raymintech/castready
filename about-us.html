About Us

Education is about encouraging dreams.

At CastReady, we believe every dreamer deserves a place to grow, belong, and shine—no matter where their journey starts. Our mission is to make acting education accessible, affordable, and empowering for all, with a hybrid approach that blends hands-on kits, digital tools, and a supportive community.

Meet Ariana: Founder & CEO

Hi, I’m Ariana Fiorello-Omotosho—a mom, educator, and lifelong believer in the power of storytelling. My journey with CastReady began when I watched my son, Kaiden, pour his heart into acting, only to find resources out of reach and progress slow despite our best efforts. After spending thousands and still feeling lost between classes, I realized families like ours deserved more. So, I created the first CastReady kit right at our kitchen table—simple, screen-free, and designed for real growth. When I shared it with other parents and industry friends, I discovered we weren’t alone. That’s when CastReady’s mission truly took shape: to build a global destination for acting education where everyone feels seen, supported, and inspired to keep growing.

Our Core Values

Our Mission

To make high-quality acting education accessible, affordable, and empowering for all—regardless of background, means, or experience.


Accessibility & Affordability

We’re committed to breaking down barriers so that every aspiring actor can access top-notch resources—no matter their zip code or budget. No one gets left out.

Growth & Empowerment

Our curriculum focuses on confidence, clear milestones, and self-assessment—so actors (and their families!) can track progress and celebrate every win, big or small.

Community & Inclusion

We intentionally create space for all voices. Our partnerships, content, and community reflect the diversity of the acting world and the real families we serve. Here, everyone belongs.

From Our Family to Yours

Every CastReady resource is designed with families in mind—by a mom who’s been there. We’re here to help you and your actor find confidence, community, and a clear path forward. Together, we’ll keep the spotlight open for every story.
